I'm

a Software Engineer, a Creator, a Musician, a Runner

Greetings! I was born in Seoul, South Korea and grew up in Michigan before studying computer science (and music) at the University of Michigan. I’m now an SDE II at AWS Glue living in NYC, where I work on large-scale data systems built on Apache Spark. I helped lead the launch of Glue v3.0, 4.0, and 5.0, which the latter supports ~22K weekly users. I also co-created an open-source MCP server for the Spark History Server to make debugging easier with AI. Ive also led a year long infra-as-code migration that reinvented the way our teams develop and test. I have a passion for organization and focus on the human aspect of engineering collaboration.

I believe Im at my best when Im building things that are technically deep but actually useful to real people. Outside of work, I am obsessed with productivity and habitualizing creativity. I write and produce music and used to lead a Korean American cover band during college where we performed at cultural shows and raised money for mutual aid. I still dabble and play guitar/sing at local events in NYC. I once built a crowd-sourced bathroom review app called Plopper because my friends and I thought it would be funny (and useful). Im fluent in Korean, recently ran a marathon, and Im always looking for ways to push myself - whether thats in code, art, relationships, or life. I care about ownership, craft, and being someone that others genuinely enjoy working with. If any of that resonates, Id love to connect!
